This is an online supplement to

Lifetime Incidence and Age of Onset of Mental Disorders, and 12-Month Service Utilization in Primary and Secondary Care



Cumulative incidence estimates the percentage of individuals who receive a mental disorder diagnosis by a certain age.

Incidence rates across the age range depict the number of people with a first-time mental disorder diagnosis per 10 000 person-years at risk.

Service utilization rate depicts the percentage of individuals aged under 100 years who had a health care visit with a mental disorder diagnosis in the year 2019and were included in the study population on December 31, 2019.
